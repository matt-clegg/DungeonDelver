
-- Level Structure

- Overworld
	- The Village
		- Contains a few populated buildings
			- General Goods Store
			- Inn
		- NPCs guide the player along, telling the player where the entrances to the dungeons are
	- The Decrepit Woods
		- The woods surrounding the village
		- Filled with vanilla creatures
		- Contains the entrances to the various dungeons.

- The dungeons
	- There are x dungeons that can be visited
	- Each dungeon has x levels and may contain some bonus branches
	- Each dungeon has a theme
